### Bio
**Dr. Kenny Felts** received his B.S. (Mathematics, 2001), M.S. (Applied Mathematics, 2003) and Ph.D (Mathematics, 2009) from the University of Missouri. He taught as a graduate student at the University of Missouri from 2001-2008 and joined Columbia College in the Fall of 2008. His wife, Kathy, works at the University of Missouri in Institutional Research. They have two children.  
  
Mathematical areas of particular interest include Dynamical Systems and Real Analysis.
