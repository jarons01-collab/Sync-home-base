When Brett Victor was working with Shawn on [his presentation](https://dynamicland.org/2023/Improvising_cellular_playgrounds_in_Realtalk/), he admitted the design of the `test tube` cards obscured their own code[^1] for the sake of a more streamlined functional design. 
Brett [admits the hidden code](https://youtu.be/nT4E5HkpLjo?t=666) is a violation of a principle, though I'm sure he and Shawn agree that it was worth it (in this instance) to make this trade-off, because if they didn't, they would be making a different trade-off, which would mean the cards would have to be bigger to accommodate the printed code.

[^1]: Each `tube` behaved identically, each serving as a container for a genetic superstructure.
