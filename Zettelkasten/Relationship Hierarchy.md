When Elder Gerrit W. Gong created his series on [Hearing God's voice in an age of Artificial Intelligence](https://youtu.be/Ts5Z64A0Vv4), he structured it around discussing the impact of AI on our relationships in the following order:
1. Our relationship with *the Divine*: [`Thou`](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ts5Z64A0Vv4&t=719s)
2. Our relationship with *oneself*: [`I`](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ts5Z64A0Vv4&t=1352s)
3. Our relationship with *other people*: [`they`](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ts5Z64A0Vv4&t=1905s)
4. Our Relationship with *the Environment and Natural World*: [`it`](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ts5Z64A0Vv4&t=2288s)
---
Could it be that our ability to maintain the health of each type of relationship on this list is subordinate to the one preceding it?
