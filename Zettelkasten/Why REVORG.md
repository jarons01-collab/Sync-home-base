The REVORG moniker designating these autonomous drones is "Grover" spelled backwards.
This is a reference to lovable, furry old Grover from [Sesame Street](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/sesame_street), who, tries in vain in a variety of ways to thwart the attempts of the reader to turn the pages of a certain Caldecott award-winning picture book, thereby reaching the end of the book where Grover discovers the titular monster he desperately tried to avoid meeting was none other than *himself*.
The agency of the reader is likewise acknowledged in this graphic novel, however, by allowing them to play the role of REVORG and deciding the eventual fate of the main character.
