At least 75\% of all collaboration is coordination.
Prior to the advent of the internet, most collaborative work was coordinated institutionally. It would have been impractical to transmit a document more than half a page in length using a telegraph.
Seemingly all online collaborative platforms require all users to have an account specific to the provider of that collaborative platform.
