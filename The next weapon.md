### Chapter 1
At this moment, the inhibitive time blade was the most dangerous weapon in the universe. The only weapons that were more powerful had been destroyed decades ago. Not many knew of the time blades existence; fewer knew of its whereabouts. Those few who did had gone into hiding with the express purpose of protecting it from those who might try to misuse its power.
At first, Cole, the master of Earth, wasn't sure what to make of the stranger who had wandered into the swamp where the time blade had been kept hidden. This person was wearing an armored robe similar to his own, though his mahogany hood didn't cover his face as Cole's did while he was engaged in a stealth mission. Distrustful of the stranger, Cole drew his bow-staff and approached him slowly.
"I see you are an elemental master?" spoke the stranger, whose remark initially took Cole by surprise. "I have met a few of your kind before; even had one or two apprenticed under me as part of a group who called ourselves the Sons of Ren."
